# Project Overview

## Business Context

This project analyzes the profitability of a Ukrainian B2B freight transportation company operating a fleet of 12 trucks between July 2022 and October 2024.

The company transports wheat, corn, sunflower, and soybeans across Ukraine. During 2024, diesel prices increased significantly while transportation tariffs remained relatively stable, creating pressure on operating margins.

## Project Objective

The objective of this project is to identify the commercial and operational factors that have the greatest impact on profitability and support data-driven business decisions.

## Business Questions

The analysis focuses on four key business questions:

1. Which clients generate the highest profit rather than just revenue?
2. How did rising fuel prices affect profitability across clients and routes?
3. Which routes have the greatest negative impact on profitability due to delays and fuel consumption?
4. Which trucks and drivers operate most efficiently?

## Analytical Workflow

The project follows an end-to-end analytics workflow:

- Synthetic dataset generation
- Relational database design
- Data quality validation
- SQL-based KPI calculation
- Exploratory data analysis
- Tableau dashboard development
- Business recommendations
